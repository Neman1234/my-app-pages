How do I report a bug?

Please email us with a description of the issue, screenshots if available, and the device you are using.

We welcome suggestions from parents, educators, and students. Send your ideas to our support email.


Why is the AI giving an unexpected response?

AI-generated content may occasionally be inaccurate. If you encounter a concerning response, please report it so we can continue improving the experience.
